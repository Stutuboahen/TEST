Our Story

Exceptional Pastries Since 2015

At Holly Wood Snacks, we've perfected the art of pastry-making through years of dedication to quality and flavor. Our convenient location adjacent to the Koforidua Forestry Department makes us the perfect stop for delicious, freshly-baked treats.

Every morning, our master bakers prepare our signature meat pies, flaky spring rolls, delicate mini cakes and other specialties using only the finest ingredients. We take pride in traditional recipes while innovating to create memorable taste experiences.
